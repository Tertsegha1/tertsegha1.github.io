How this works

  1. Work through each week in order — every week teaches a concept through a real-world scenario, with a live editor to practise in.
  2. Pass the short quiz and complete the practice exercises to unlock the next week.
  3. Along the way you'll tackle puzzle-style mini projects that combine everything so far.
  4. Finish the final mini project to unlock your level certificate — and the next level.

🌱 Level 1 — Beginner: Meet Your Data

DataFrames, averages, and filtering

What you'll build

Across this level you'll analyse real school data with pandas — start with exam scores (counting, averaging, filtering), then build up to selecting/sorting/grouping, handling missing data, merging tables, and a final Sports Day results analysis capstone.

🌿 Level 2 — Intermediate: Grouping & Sorting

Combining and organising bigger datasets

What you'll build

Analyse school data end-to-end with pandas: select and filter rows, combine conditions, sort into a leaderboard, group and aggregate by category, add computed columns, handle missing data, merge two related tables, and summarise a whole dataset — the same 9-week course as Beginner, reused here for now.
